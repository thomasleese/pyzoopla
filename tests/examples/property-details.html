No onward chain

This unique well presented, semi-detached house is located within walking distance to Bracknell town centre. The location provides easy access to local schools and amenities as well as Bracknell train station and the M3/M4.

The accommodation comprises a 21ft living/dining room which has double doors leading onto the rear enclosed garden, spacious kitchen/breakfast room and cloakroom.

To the first floor you will find four bedrooms, two of them being double bedrooms and a family bathroom.

Further benefits include a tandem garage, off-road parking for four vehicles and a 150ft enclosed rear garden.

There is scope to extend the property to the side and rear (STPP).
